About Me

I'm Tony Shen, a data professional and developer with expertise in data analysis, visualization, and web development. With a background in political science and data science, I bring a unique perspective to problem-solving and creating data-driven solutions.

My Journey

2020 – Started My Career

I began my journey into tech as a software intern at a local startup, where I developed my foundational skills in data analysis and programming.

2022 – Joined US HUD

As a Data Analyst at the US Department of Housing and Urban Development, I worked on significant projects analyzing housing data and creating actionable insights for policy decisions.

2023 – Completed Master's Degree

Graduated with an M.S. in Data Science from Georgia Tech, specializing in machine learning and predictive analytics.

2024 – Expanded into Full-Stack Development

Broadened my skill set to include full-stack web development, creating data-driven web applications that transform complex information into accessible insights.

2025 – Present

Currently exploring new opportunities where I can leverage my combined expertise in data science and web development to create impactful solutions.
